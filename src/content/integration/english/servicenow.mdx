### Automated Incident Creation [.text-h5]

Automatically create ServiceNow incidents and security cases when critical vulnerabilities are detected by our AI Red Team platform.

### Vulnerability Management Workflow [.text-h5]

Streamline vulnerability remediation processes by integrating with ServiceNow's vulnerability response workflows and change management systems.

### Risk Assessment Integration [.text-h5]

Combine vulnerability data with ServiceNow's risk assessment framework to prioritize remediation efforts based on business impact.

### Compliance Tracking [.text-h5]

Track compliance remediation efforts and generate audit reports using ServiceNow's governance, risk, and compliance (GRC) capabilities.

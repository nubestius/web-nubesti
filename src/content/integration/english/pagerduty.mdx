### Critical Security Alerting [.text-h5]

Instantly alert your security team when critical vulnerabilities or active threats are detected, with intelligent routing to the right personnel.

### Escalation Management [.text-h5]

Automatically escalate unacknowledged security incidents through your defined escalation paths, ensuring no critical issue goes unnoticed.

### Incident Response Coordination [.text-h5]

Coordinate incident response efforts with automated notifications, status updates, and communication bridges during security emergencies.

### On-Call Scheduling [.text-h5]

Integrate with your existing on-call schedules to ensure security incidents are routed to available team members 24/7.
